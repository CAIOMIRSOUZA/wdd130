Know more about our trips

Rapids Rush Adventure

Embark on a thrilling journey through roaring rapids and scenic landscapes. This adrenaline-pumping adventure takes you through challenging rapids, tranquil stretches, and picturesque canyons. Suitable for both beginners and experienced rafters, Rapids Rush Adventure promises an unforgettable experience in the heart of nature.

Wilderness Expedition

Dive into the heart of wilderness on this immersive rafting expedition. Over the course of five days, you'll navigate through remote rivers, camp under the stars, and explore untouched landscapes. Led by experienced guides, Wilderness Expedition offers a perfect blend of excitement, exploration, and relaxation amidst pristine natural beauty.

Family Fun Float

Create lasting memories with your family on this fun-filled rafting trip. Designed for families with children, Family Fun Float offers gentle rapids, calm waters, and stunning scenery. Spend quality time together while floating down the river, enjoying picnics on the riverbank, and bonding over campfire stories under the starlit sky.
